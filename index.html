Homonculus

here is a brief discription of the homonculus

Homunculus are artificial humans created by Father, each embodying one of the seven deadly sins. They are characterized by their near-immortality, fueled by Philosopher's Stones, and regenerative abilities. They possess enhanced strength, speed, and agility, and can heal from almost any injury as long as their Philosopher's Stone has power. However, they are not truly immortal; their Philosopher's Stones can be depleted, leading to their death.

Here is a brief description of each Homunculus in order.

He takes the form of a small kid named "Selim Bradley" and he manifests his powers as shadows that can turn solid, he also can take a shadowy form. Lust: She is the second Homunculus that Father. She takes the form of an attractive pale-skinned woman with wavy black long hair, her ability is turning her fingers into long sharp blade like, she's also known for manipulating men with her beauty.
Gluttony: He is the third Homunculus, he was born of a failed experiment, in which Father tried to recreate the door of the truth, Gluttony is a big guy in overweight, and he has the mental age of a little kid. He has an incredile strength and he can open his belly to show the door of the truth which leads to a whole other dimention. Greed: The fourth child of father. He is very tall and buff, and his face seems emotionless with a sign of fatigue. Envy: The last Homunculus.
